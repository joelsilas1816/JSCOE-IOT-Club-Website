Objectives of the IOT Club

  • The objective of IoT Club is to promote an environment for the interaction between students pursuing Computer Engineering and those from other departments or domains to create an enhanced scope for interdisciplinary projects. The IoT club engages in activities related to solving the problems of the society and environment to help develop smart cities. Students participate in designing and implementing different IoT projects for the same. IoT Club is the undergraduate students group of the Department of Computer Engineering.
  • It provides students the necessary platform for realizing their ideas. A great workspace, a comprehensive inventory, ample guidance from faculty, senior students, Alumni, and also the industrial experts. IOT club is divided based on interests, each club represents a highly enthusiastic community motivated to spread and improve the knowledge base of the field. Our aim is to enhance the students' project quality, placements, participation in research project competitions & to motivate students towards research, innovation & Higher Studies.

153+ PLACEMENTS


The Placement Cell plays a crucial role in proving job opportunities for all Under Graduates students passing out from the college by being connected with reputed firms and industrial establishments and helps them in recruiting our students.

This companies visit campus and conduct Campus placement drives and the number of students placed through the campus interviews is continuously rising. Successfully maintaining high placement statistics over the years is a testimony to our quality.

The Placement Cell arranges training programmes like Mock Interviews, Group Discussions, Communication Skills Workshop etc and it also organises Public Sector Exam Training for students who are interested to join Government Sectors.
